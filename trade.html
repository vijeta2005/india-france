What is traded?

Both countries export very different things — which is exactly why the trade relationship works so well.

🇮🇳 India exports to France

  • Pharmaceuticals & medicines
  • Textiles & garments
  • IT & software services
  • Gems & jewellery
  • Engineering goods
  • Chemicals

🇫🇷 France exports to India

  • Aircraft & aerospace parts
  • Luxury goods & cosmetics
  • Machinery & equipment
  • Nuclear reactor components
  • Electrical equipment
  • Wine & food products
French companies in India

Over 1,000 French companies have a presence in India — here are some you definitely know.

AB

Airbus

Supplies commercial aircraft to Air India, IndiGo, and other Indian airlines.

L'O

L'Oréal

One of the largest beauty brands in India, with local manufacturing plants.

RN

Renault

Makes and sells cars in India. The Kwid was designed specifically for Indian roads.

TT

Total Energies

Major investor in India's oil, gas, and renewable energy sector.

BN

BNP Paribas

One of France's largest banks, with significant operations across India.

SC

Schneider Electric

Provides energy management solutions to Indian industries and infrastructure.

Did you know?

The Renault Kwid — one of India's bestselling small cars — was designed in France but engineered entirely for Indian roads, Indian fuel quality, and Indian price points. It was Renault's first car built ground-up for an emerging market, and it became a massive success.
